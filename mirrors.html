SPHERICAL MIRRORS

1. Introduction

Concave mirror (converging) & Convex mirror (diverging). Used in headlights, shaving mirrors, rear-view mirrors.

2. Sign Convention (Cartesian) – Most Important for Exams

QuantitySign
Object distance (u)Always Negative
Focal length (f)Concave = Negative, Convex = Positive
Image distance (v)Real = Negative, Virtual = Positive

3. Mirror Formula

\[ \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u} \]

4. Magnification

\[ m = -\frac{v}{u} = \frac{h_i}{h_o} \]
